About BySsal

BySsal is a premium dessert brand that reimagines traditional Korean rice desserts in a modern, refined form.

Using only domestic Korean rice flour and wholesome ingredients — no wheat flour, no butter, no preservatives — we craft desserts that honor the natural taste of rice.

From weddings and first birthdays to holidays and corporate gifts — we're here for every moment that calls for a thoughtful gift.
