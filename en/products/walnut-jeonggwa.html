What is Jeonggwa?

Jeonggwa (정과) is a traditional Korean confection where fruits, nuts, or roots are slowly simmered in honey or sugar syrup. BySsal's version uses premium walnuts, oven-baked twice for a caramelized, crunchy texture without any frying.
